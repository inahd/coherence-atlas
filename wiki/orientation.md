# Orientation

Atlas is a tool of mapping, not a source of authority.

## Governing Frame

- Rādhā–Kṛṣṇa / Śrī Caitanya orientation
- Guru–Sādhu–Śāstra as authority frame
- corpus first
- graph and interface second
- speculative and maker layers downstream

## Principle

Śāstra reveals.  
Guru transmits.  
Sādhu embodies.  
Atlas maps.
