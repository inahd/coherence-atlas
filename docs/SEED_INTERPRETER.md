# Atlas Seed Interpreter

The Seed Interpreter is responsible for translating raw creative seeds into structured Atlas artifacts.

Seeds may originate from:
- research prompts
- creative riffing
- cosmological reflection
- software design
- cultural reconstruction ideas

The interpreter assigns each seed four structural properties.

---

## 1. COSMIC LAYER

Where the seed lives in the cosmological model.

CIT          consciousness
SABDA        sound / meaning
GUNA         energetic qualities
TATTVA       philosophical principle
RUPA         form / geometry
LIFE         biology / ecology
KALA         time cycles
SOCIAL       culture / institutions
INTERFACE    tools / software
MYTHIC       symbolic narrative

---

## 2. EPISTEMIC MODE

How the knowledge is obtained.

DESCENDING      scriptural or traditional revelation
ASCENDING       empirical observation
COMMENTARIAL    interpretation of prior texts
SPECULATIVE     hypothesis
PRACTICAL       applied system

---

## 3. THESIS ROLE

What function the seed serves in the Atlas thesis.

AXIS            core philosophical principle
BRIDGE          conceptual connection
CASE            example or study
METHOD          research approach
APPLICATION     practical system
VISION          future direction

---

## 4. COSMIC EGG ZONE

The development stage of the seed.

SHELL           project boundary or architecture
YOLK            central thesis core
ALBUMEN         supportive conceptual field
HATCHING        emerging applications

---

## Example Interpreted Seed

Title: Consciousness → Sound → Form

COSMIC_LAYER: SABDA
EPISTEMIC_MODE: DESCENDING
THESIS_ROLE: AXIS
COSMIC_EGG_ZONE: YOLK

Interpretation:
This seed belongs to the central cosmological argument of the Atlas and should anchor multiple downstream nodes.
