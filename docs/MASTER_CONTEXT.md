This document is the **canonical context injection** for new collaborators (human or AI) entering Coherence Atlas.

Atlas is a **relational cosmology / knowledge graph environment** for mapping living knowledge ecosystems.

Atlas does not merely store facts. It maps:

- entities
- relations
- symbols
- practices
- cycles
- texts
- cosmologies
- research seeds

The graph is the truth layer. Everything else is a projection.

## 3. Cosmology Model

Atlas supports three simultaneous cosmological layers:

- eternal
- subtle
- material

These layers must not be collapsed into one. They may resonate across layers.

Example resonance:

- eternal: Krishna, Radha, Goloka, lila, rasa

## 4. Graph Principle

Atlas does not decide what a thing “really is.” It records how different systems describe it.

Example — Mars:

- astronomy → planet

- mythology → deity / archetype

Same node, multiple relational descriptions.
