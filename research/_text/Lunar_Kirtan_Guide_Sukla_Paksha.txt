How to Use This Guide
Sukla Paksha emphasizes the Perfect Fifth (Sa to Pa). Build melodic arcs upward, gradually increase
rhythmic density toward Purnima, and reduce intensity on Ekadasi. Use this guide as a musical contour
map rather than rigid prescription.
